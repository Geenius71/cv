Kettlebell Training

Too many poor trainers and trainees using these beasts with ugly form. If your trainer doesn't have a specific KB certification (and I'm not talking of Kettlercise or similar), avoid them.

These are a fantastic tool for getting stronger and in shape. But, in the wrong hands, they are potentially dangerous. Use wisely, use carefully, and seek professional help.
